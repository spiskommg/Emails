Easy, healthy, delicious snack and meal ideas for your kids
- Pack a thermos with organic Black Bean Soup
- Make a kid-favorite wrap with 100% sustainably caught WildPink Salmon
- Pack organic Fruit & Almond Bars or grass-fed Buffalo Jerky for go-to snacks during the day
Ingredients

- Pink salmon
- Medium sized tortilla
- Cream cheese
- Cucumber
- Micro greens
- Optional: herbs, capers

On a flat work surface lay out your favorite medium sized tortilla. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese covering the entire tortilla. Along the center lay some greens and thin slices of cucumber. Gently place one packet of pink salmon along the middle leaving space at the bottom edge.

Top with micro greens, herbs and capers if you’d like. Fold up the bottom edge first, then starting from one end, firmly wrap up like a burrito pulling that salmon in tight. The cream cheese will make it all stick together. You can leave like a burrito and wrap it up in our reusable & washable Bee’s Wrap, or slice into rounds and put in your favorite container for a healthy lunch.
